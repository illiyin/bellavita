EYELASH EXTENSIONS & EYEBROW TINTING

At Bella Vita, we specialize in safe and customized eyelash extensions. Our exclusive technique tailors to each individual’s unique eye shape using only premium synthetic materials that are curled to imitate a natural lash. Our eyelash extensions provide a rich-looking appearance that eliminates the need for mascara, giving you a low-maintenance beauty routine.

EYEBROW TINTING

For a quick pick-me-up, eyebrow tints offer a beautiful pop of color that lasts up to two weeks. Combine tinting with a precision-style eyebrow waxing and you will be amazed at the instant youthful appearance it provides.

CLASSIC PEDICURE

Relax and balance your feet with our spa pedicure treatment, full of key ingredients to enrich your skin with beneficial nutrients. The classic pedicure includes a sea salt soak, nail reshape, cuticle details, exfoliating scrub, massage with a moisturizing lotion, and a polish color of your choice.

LAVISH PEDICURE

Relieve your tensions and take your pedicure experience to the next level. This deluxe spa pedicure is a four-step treatment with specific ingredients to give your feet valuable nutrients. Each pedicure uses individually wrapped products to ensure a sanitary and hygienic session every time. The lavish spa pedicure includes a warm deep sea salt soak, nail reshape, cuticle detailing, exfoliating sugar scrub, callus remover, hydrating mud mask wrapped in hot towels, massage with a unique moisturizer lotion, and finish with a polish of your choice.

INEFFABLE PEDICURE

For the ultimate indulgence, pamper yourself to a well-deserved treat! This “too good to describe in words” ineffable spa pedicure will relax muscles, relieve blocked energy, reduce calluses, and diminish dry and rough skin. Relieve your tensions with this spa pedicure by relaxing in our massage chair while your feet are given a warm deep sea salt soak of your choice. We will then trim and reshape your nails, cuticle detail, exfoliating sugar scrub, callus remover, hydrating mud masque wrapped in hot towels, extended massage with a unique moisturizing lotion, collagen wrapped treatment to richly hydrate and condition feet, and finish with a polish of your choice.

Bella Vita Today!

Our goal is to provide a fun and relaxing atmosphere for a spa experience that everyone can afford! We offer services that include: eyelashes, permanent make up, nails, massages, skincare, waxing, and hair. We are a MMA Free Salon! Files and buffers are not reused and each customer gets a pack used only once. Our spa chairs are also PIPE-LESS, which means the water is not re-circulated through whirlpool pipes, ensuring all parts of the spa bowl are bacteria free and clean. All tubs are lined with a clean, sanitized plastic liner for each and every customer, guaranteeing extreme cleanliness. We are dedicated to providing you with the most natural and sanitary nail care available. All of our implements are sterilized using hospital-grade procedures to ensure your health and safety.

What sets us apart from other salons is that we are truly a one stop shop as we provide a nice selection of clothes and accessories in our boutique at great prices!
